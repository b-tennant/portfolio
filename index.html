About me

My name's Benjamin Tennant and I'm from Staffordshire, not too far from Birmingham, UK. After excelling in Economics at A level, I decided to continue reading the subject at University. Before going to University, I took a medical gap year. During this time out I under-went two rounds of complex knee surgery following a nasty rugby injury, as well as a brief period of travel. I worked part-time at Waitrose throughout the entirity of my degree and went on to gain upper second class honours. Outside of term time I completed two finance internships, one of which was at Barclays within their Corporate Bank.

Continual development of my commercial awareness led me to recognise the growing dependancy upon tech to drive growth and efficiency within businesses. With this in mind, as well as the COVID-19 job market, I decided to enroll on an intensive Software Development training programme with _nology. This allows me to upskill, by gaining future-proof programming skills, working with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Node.js and React.js. While on the course I will also be involved in a client project.
